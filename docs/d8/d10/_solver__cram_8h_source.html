9 /*!
10  * \class Origen::Solver_cram
11  * \brief CRAM depletion solver class satisfying Origen::solver.
12  * \author Aarno Isotalo
13  *
14  * \sa Origen::Solver, kernel_cram()
15  *
16  * The options for the CRAM solver are:
17  * - "cramOrder" (int)
18  * - The order of the cram approximation.
19  * - Must be an even integer between 4 and 16.
20  * - Default: 16.
21  * - "cramInternalSubsteps" (int)
22  * - The number of internal substeps in CRAM.
23  * - Must be a positive integer.
24  * - Default: 1.
25  * - "isAdjoint" (bool)
26  * - Is this an adjoint calculation?
27  * - Can be true or false.
28  * - Default: false.
29  * - "solver" (string)
30  * - Which solver are the options for? Used for some consistency checks.
31  * - Must be "cram".
32  * - Default: "cram".
33  * - "cramRemoveNegatives" (int)
34  * - Whether any negative values in the results should be set to zero.
35  * - Can be:
36  * - 0: Do not do anything.
37  * - 1: Remove all negatives. Note that some adjoint calculations
38  * may intentionally involve negative input and hence
39  * negative-but-still-physical output.
40  * - 2: Remove all negatives in forward calculations. Remove all
41  * negatives in adjoint calculations if:
42  * - All initial concentrations are non-negative 0,
43  * - All feed rates are non-negative, AND
44  * - Source term is constant.
45  * - Default: 2
46  * - "cramResultsCutoff" (double)
47  * - Concentrations whose absolute value is below
48  * cramCutoff*sum(abs(n_final)) are returned as zero.
49  * - Must be non-negative.
50  * - Default: 0.0 (does nothing)
51  * - "tallyEnergyReleased" (bool)
52  * - Whether to add a tally nuclide to collect the amount of energy
53  * released during the depletion step.
54  * - With non zero flux, uses the reaction kappas that include decay
55  * - With zero flux, uses decay q-values.
56  * - Note that if you sum depletion and subsequent decay steps, the
57  * decay energy will be counted twice (as it was already rolled to
58  * the reaction kappas),
59  * - The result is found in status()->get<double>("energyReleased") and
60  * is in [MeV]/[units of n0]
61  * - Default: false.
62  * - "tallyNumFissions" (bool)
63  * - Whether to add a tally nuclide to collect the number of neutron
64  * induced fissions.
65  * - The result is found in status()->get<double>("numFissions") and is
66  * in the units of n0.
67  * - Default: false.
68  * - "tallyNumCaptures" (bool)
69  * - Whether to add a tally nuclide to collect the total number of
70  * neutron induced removal reactions (including fissions).
71  * - The result is found in status()->get<double>("numCaptures") and is
72  * in the units of n0.
73  * - Default: false.
74  * - "tallyAveConcentrations" (bool)
75  * - Whether to add a tally nuclides to collect the average concentrations
76  * of ALL nuclides in the library.
77  * - The results are found in status()->get<double>("ave_<ZZZAAAI>")
78  * and is in the units of n0. <ZZZAAAI> is the extended ZAID of the
79  * nuclide (leading zeroes ommited).
80  * - Default: false.
81  *
82  * The default values are returned by Solver::get_defaults() if the Solver is
83  * Solver_cram. They can also be retreived with the static function
84  * Solver_cram::get_defaults_static(). The default values should not be hard
85  * coded anywhere else than this class.
86  */
